Review

Alice Lemonade

I almost passed out

I don't think I've ever eaten anything so good. Nope ! I have never
eaten anything so good. First of all I think we can talk about the
location of the restaurant which is juste wonderful. Yes you are not dreaming
the restaurant is located under water which offers us a breathtaking view !
The atmosphere is amazing, yes you can eat while watching the sharks swim.
But this is nothing compared to what I am about to tell you.
There are no staff! We order directly on the tables with integrated
screens and a robot brings us the meal in less than a minute!!! Finally,
the secret pill, when I ate it, my body shook and I felt a sensation
never seen before. Yes I have never eaten anything as good as that.
Despite its price of 100 euros it costs you, so go for it!!!

Christopher Ronald

The crocodile paw scared me !

My wife and I, we were very impressed by these robots. They
brought us the meal in 1 minute, which is very rewarding for a
restaurant. My wife was very frightened by these sharks which
circled around us while we were eating. Overall the restaurant is
very well done, there is the corner for a drink, which has a very
futuristic look and the place to eat which located under water.
Personally, I really liked this view! But came the moment of the
dish. I ordered the restaurant's specialty, that is to say the crocodile
paw and I received a real crocodile paw! I was very dripped and
scared but when I tasted it I had a taste orgasm, it was excellent. I
don't give 5 stars because of the crocodile paw which was visually
very disgusting. The prices of the specialties are very expensive but I highly recommend this restaurant.
